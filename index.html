Q450 Glass Fiber Quadcopter Frame - Integrated PCB Version

This is a sturdy 49.5cm diameter Quad rotor frame with power tracks on PCB for directly connecting ESCs to Battery. This feature greatly simplifies the electronics layout and makes your platform more reliable. Arms are made up of ultra durable polyamide nylon. Arms have brass inserts with M3 screw threads. Arms can survive repeated crashes without breaking. This Quad Frame is shipped in unassembled kit form.

Lithium Polymer 3 Cell, 11.1V, 3300mAh, 20C discharge Battery

This is a high performance 3 Cell, 11.1V, 3300mAh, 20C Battery. It can give discharge current of 66Amps. It has a ‘T’ type Power connector and a 4 pin JST connector for battery charging. It is an ideal battery for robots which require high current.

Protection Circuit for 3 Cell Li-Po Battery

Protection Circuit for 3 Cell Li-Po Battery is a microcontroller based smart circuit for protecting battery from deep discharge and becoming unstable if discharged below 9V. Smart battery chargers like NR-BLIC-B6AC and NR-BLIC-03 will not charge the 3 Cell Li-Po batteries if they discharged below 9V for safety reasons. Protection Circuit shows battery level in 4 steps and gives audio warning if battery is discharged below critical level. Use this battery protection circuit to protect your valuable battery from deep discharging and becoming unusable.

20Amp BLDC ESC

This is fully programmable 20A BLDC ESC with 5V, 2A BEC. Can drive motors with continuous 20Amp load current. It has sturdy construction with heatsaink on the MOSFETs for better heat dissipation. It can be powered with 2-4 lithium Polymer batteries or 5-12 NiMH / NiCd batteries. It has separate voltage regulator for the microcontroller for providing good anti-jamming capability. It is most suitable for UAVs, Aircrafts and Helicopters.

PolyMax 3.5mm Gold Connectors PAIR

3.5mm Gold connector. Rated to approx. 80A.Ideal for connecting ESCs with BLDC motors.Allows flexibility of changing motor direction very easily.

B6AC Universal Battery Charger

B6AC Universal Battery Charger can charge 2 to 6 Cell Lithium Polymer battery packs, 1 to 15 NiMH / NiCd battery packs, and 2V to 12V Lead Acid Battery. The charging current is user programmable between 0.1 to 6Amps. It also features a balance charger for Lithium Polymer Battery chemistry where it balances battery with drain current of up to 300mA per cell. It has very simple interactive user interface based on 2x16 alphanumeric LCD along with 4 menu switches. It also includes charging cables for 5 different types of battery sockets. This charger works on mains supply as well as 11 to 18V DC.

Control Systems

Feedback is the central concept of life. From natural processes to man-made processes, we can witness power of feedback almost everywhere. For example, in human body pancreas continuously monitors glucose level and accordingly secrets insulin. This helps in maintaining the sugar level in body. Taking example of man-made system, if we want a robot to reach a desired position, we continuously monitor its current position and compare it with desired position. According to the difference, we have algorithms which forces robot to reach its desired position. Control Systems and State Estimation are integral part of these feedback systems.

Control Systems

As witnessed from the above diagram, feedback is central to our decision making. We need some means to infer our current status, compare it with our desired goals and accordingly our mind generates certain actions. In man-made systems and processes, Control System takes place of mind and State Estimation takes place of inferring current state.

Why exactly Control Systems??

  • To provide intelligence and automation to system/process
  • To stabilize a system
  • To manipulate its dynamic response

Let us take example of quadcopter and elucidate above points. Quadcopter has four motors which act as its actuators. Inherently quadcopter is unstable owing to the intrinsic differences in dynamics of four motors. Quadcopter needs to have algorithm which can command it to remain in horizontal position. This intelligence and stability is provided by control theory. Also intrinsic response time of quadcopter might not be acceptable for our applicaton. We might need quadcopter to reach desired height in particular time, but it may take longer. This dynamic response can also be manipulated by control theory.

Thus, it is evident that control systems form very critical part in every system. Now we face a question that if control systems are so much vital, then where are they found?? The answer is they are ubiquitous, found everywhere. From the pointer of disk drive in your laptop to the spacecrafts which makes a complete autonomous mission in space.
